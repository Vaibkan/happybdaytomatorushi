# Audio Files Guide

Add your audio files to this `audio/` folder.

## Required Sound Effects

Create or download these sound effect files (MP3 format recommended):

1. **click.mp3** - Sound when clicking buttons
2. **hover.mp3** - Sound when hovering over buttons
3. **heart.mp3** - Sound when clicking the heart icon
4. **slide.mp3** - Sound when navigating slides
5. **gift.mp3** - Sound when opening the gift box
6. **achievement.mp3** - Sound when unlocking achievements
7. **confetti.mp3** - Sound for confetti effects
8. **sparkle.mp3** - Sound for sparkle effects
9. **popup.mp3** - Sound when popups appear

## Background Music (Optional)

1. **romantic-bg.mp3** - Background music for the Romantic Route
2. **rizzy-bg.mp3** - Background music for the Rizzy Route

## Where to Find Free Audio Files

- **FreeSound.org** (freesound.org) - Free sound effects
- **Zapsplat.com** - Free sound effects library
- **Pixabay.com** - Free music and sound effects
- **YouTube Audio Library** - Free music tracks

## File Formats

- **MP3** - Recommended (best compatibility)
- **OGG** - Also supported
- **WAV** - Supported but larger file sizes

## Tips

- Keep sound effects short (0.5-2 seconds)
- Background music should loop seamlessly
- Use lower volume for background music
- Test on mobile devices to ensure sounds work

## If You Don't Have Audio Files

The website will work without audio files - it will just silently fail to load sounds. You can add them later when you have them ready!
